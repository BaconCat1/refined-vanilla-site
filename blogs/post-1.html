The Tricky Trials (1.21) has arrived!

Update • June 15, 2024

We are thrilled to announce that Refined Vanilla has officially updated to Minecraft 1.21. This update brings a massive amount of content without compromising our "Pure Vanilla" philosophy.

What's New?

The main highlight of this update is the Trial Chamber. These are massive, procedurally generated underground structures filled with challenges and unique rewards.

"The history of the server is written by its players. Version 1.21 gives you more tools than ever to leave your mark."

Technical Improvements

To support the new version, we have upgraded our hardware to the latest Ryzen 9 processors. This ensures that even with the increased chunk data in 1.21, the server maintains a stable 20 TPS.
