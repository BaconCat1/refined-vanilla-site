Server Rules

Refined Vanilla is an Anarchy server, but we enforce specific rules to protect the community and the stability of the platform.

  • No Illegal Content or Real-World Harm No doxxing, real-world threats, terrorism, or any illegal activity. Do not create builds, structures, or any other content relating to harmful ideas, hateful symbols, or hateful speech.
  • No Impersonation Do not impersonate staff, Mojang Studios, or Microsoft. Do not claim the server is official or endorsed.
  • No Hacked Clients or Cheats All hacked clients, cheats, and unfair modifications are prohibited. Performance and basic QoL mods are allowed (e.g., OptiFine, simple minimaps).
  • Duplication Rules Allowed: TNT, rail, and carpet duplication. Not allowed: All other duplication methods or bug abuse.
  • No Server-Killing Behavior No lag machines, crash exploits, or anything intended to harm server stability.
  • No Advertising or Real-Money Trading No excessive advertising. No selling items or accounts for real money.
  • Chat Disclaimer and Content Warning Player chat is not actively moderated at all times and may contain language or content that some users find offensive. Refined Vanilla does not endorse, promote, or condone offensive, hateful, or inappropriate language used by players. By playing on this server, you acknowledge that you may encounter unfiltered player-generated chat.
  • Staff Discretion Staff may act as needed to enforce these rules or comply with platform policies. Open a ticket in support-tickets if you need help.
  • IP Responsibility You are responsible for your IP address. If you share an IP address with a banned player, you will also be banned. As such, use VPNs at your own risk.

By playing on Refined Vanilla, you agree to these rules. Have fun and enjoy your time!
