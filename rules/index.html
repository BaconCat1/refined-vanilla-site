Server Rules

Refined Vanilla is an Anarchy server, but we enforce specific rules to ensure the technical stability of the platform and the safety of our community.

  • No Hacking or Exploiting Using hacked clients, cheats, or exploits to gain an unfair advantage is strictly forbidden. Quality-of-life mods like OptiFine or minimaps are allowed. Auto-clickers are allowed for farm usage.
  • No Duplication / Bug Abuse TNT, rail, and carpet duplication are allowed. Any other duplication or bug abuse is likely patched and not permitted.
  • No Doxxing Sharing private or personal information of any player will result in a permanent ban with no appeal.
